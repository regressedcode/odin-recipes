Scrambled Eggs

How to make the best scrambled eggs

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Salt, Pepper
  • optional: Green Onion

Steps

  1. Put your pan at medium heat
  2. Add the butter
  3. Add 2-3 eggs
  4. With a wooden spatula keep moving the eggs
  5. Take off the heat once u have ur desired consistency
