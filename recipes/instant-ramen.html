Instant Ramen

Quick and simple Ramen-recipe to ease your appetite in only 3 minutes!

Ingredients

  • just a cup of instant noodles will be needed here

How to make your instant noodles

  1. Heat up your water with a water heater or in any pot on ur stove
  2. Once the water is at boiling point u can open your noodle cup
  3. Make sure to open all the spices and put them inside the cup
  4. Finally, pour in the water up to the designated mark inside the cup, enjoy!
